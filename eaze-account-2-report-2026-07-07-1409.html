Executive summary

Across what is now a nearly-complete Sun–Mon week (Jul 5–6; Tuesday has not yet started on the PST8PDT advertiser clock), Eaze Account 2 spent $1,332.88 across three active campaigns, producing 26 med spa appointments at a blended $45.54 each and 8 dental leads at $18.60 each — both blended figures computed by dividing total spend for each result type by its result count, carried to four decimal places before rounding. Instagram absorbed 56.7% of spend ($755.50 of $1,332.88) at 1.92% CTR, versus Facebook's 3.38% CTR on $540.81 — again nearly double the engagement rate for about two-thirds of the budget. iPhone alone carries 77.7% of spend ($1,036.27) at a real efficiency discount to Android smartphone (2.34% CTR / $6.40 CPC vs. 3.92% CTR / $2.84 CPC). All three flagship appointment ads again posted a lower CTR on Monday than Sunday, and with Monday now almost fully elapsed rather than partial, this is the same direction seen in the earlier pull today — still only two daily data points, so this reads as an early signal worth watching, not confirmed fatigue.

  • Best appointment engine: Aashish Maru UGC Reels (CBO, Demographic) at $31.31 per appointment, 14 appointments — 49.6% cheaper per result than the ABO Med Spa + Dental campaign ($62.15/appointment).
  • Best lead engine: RPF 01 - Dentists at $18.60 per lead, 8 leads — the account's only lead-generating campaign this window.
  • Biggest drain: six near-zero orphan ads (UGC Reel Ad 03, V1 | AI Image 1 | Dentist, V1 | Image 1 | Med Spa, V1 | AI Image 2 | Med Spa, UGC Reel Ad 01, UGC Reel Ad 04) — $29.69 combined spend, zero results across both days. This is the same group and the same figure used again in section 03 and section 12 below.
  • Notable change: spend continued climbing on the same Sun–Mon window (+$207.71 vs. the last run) with 3 new appointments (all from the UGC Reels campaign) and leads holding flat at 8; the opportunity score held flat at 81/100.

Action list

  1. Apply Meta's A+ Creative enhancements bundle across the account. It's the single highest-leverage recommendation available (7-point opportunity score lift, 3% lower cost per result) and already covers nearly every active ad.
  2. Let Meta auto-add music to the 3 eligible ads for a further 2-point score lift and ~5% lower cost per result, at no creative cost.
  3. Rebalance platform delivery away from Instagram. Facebook runs 3.38% CTR at $3.63 CPC vs Instagram's 1.92% CTR at $8.39 CPC, yet Instagram holds 56.7% of spend ($755.50 vs $540.81).
  4. Shift budget toward Android where efficiency allows. Android smartphone runs 3.92% CTR at $2.84 CPC vs iPhone's 2.34% CTR at $6.40 CPC, but iPhone still carries 77.7% of spend.
  5. Retire or refresh the six zero-result orphan ads (UGC Reel Ad 03, V1 | AI Image 1 | Dentist, V1 | Image 1 | Med Spa, V1 | AI Image 2 | Med Spa, UGC Reel Ad 01, UGC Reel Ad 04) — $29.69 combined spend with no results across two days.
